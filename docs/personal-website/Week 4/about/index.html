Helpful Tips#

  • Use facts, not hype.
  • Seek to inspire trust.
  • Tell visitors (recruiters, friends you want to brag to) what they want to know.
  • Specifics are better than bold claims.
  • Give a peek behind the curtains.
  • Express your values.
